## MVP scope (as confirmed)
- HRMS + spreadsheets + meeting transcripts are mandatory inputs.
- In-meeting assistance is required.
- Single organization (design for scaling later).

## Minimal UX (exactly what we build)
- Dashboard: employee select + quick overview
- Employee profile: unified profile + timeline + prep brief + open loops
- Conversational assistant: grounded Q&A with citations
- In-meeting assistance panel: real-time prompts with citations
