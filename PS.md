PROBLEM STATEMENT-2 
CXO HR Intelligence Dashboard 
Description: 
Senior HR leaders such as Chief HR Officers (CHROs) are responsible not only for managing 
organisational policies but also for maintaining meaningful relationships with employees. 
However, in modern organisations with hundreds or thousands of employees, personal and 
contextual information about employees is scattered across multiple systems such as HRMS 
platforms, spreadsheets, meeting transcripts, and email conversations. 
Because these systems operate independently, HR leaders often lack the institutional memory 
required to remember past conversations, personal interests, or important employee 
concerns. As a result, quarterly check-ins and leadership conversations become generic and 
transactional rather than meaningful and relationship-driven. 
This challenge requires participants to build an AI-powered HR Intelligence Dashboard that 
aggregates employee information from multiple sources such as HRMS systems, 
spreadsheets, and meeting transcripts. The system should construct a unified employee 
profile that includes both official data and relevant contextual insights extracted from past 
interactions. 
The platform should include a conversational AI assistant that helps HR leaders prepare for 
employee check-ins, recall previous discussions, generate conversation starters, and 
optionally provide contextual suggestions during live meetings. The goal is to help HR leaders 
engage with employees in a more informed, empathetic, and personalised way at scale. 
User: 
• Chief HR Officers (CHROs) 
• Senior HR Business Partners 
• Talent and people operations leaders 
• HR teams responsible for employee engagement and retention 
Impact: 
• Improves employee engagement by enabling more personalised and meaningful 
leadership conversations. 
• Provides institutional memory by consolidating historical employee interactions and 
contextual information. 
• Enhances HR decision-making with insights derived from employee communication 
patterns and sentiment trends. 
• Reduces preparation time for leadership check-ins and HR meetings. 
• Helps identify early signals of employee concerns, improving retention and workplace 
culture. 
Tech that can be used: 
• AI/LLMs for conversational assistants and summarisation 
• Natural Language Processing for transcript analysis and insight extraction 
• HRMS API integrations (Zoho People, BambooHR, Freshteam, etc.) 
• Speech-to-text and transcript processing tools 
• Secure backend systems for employee profile management 
• Full-stack web dashboards (React, Node.js, Python, etc.) 
• Cloud infrastructure and secure data storage
